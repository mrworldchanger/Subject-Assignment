Audio and Its Types

Audio is a term used to describe any sound or noise that is within a range the human ear is capable of hearing. Measured in hertz, the audio signal on a computer is generated using a sound card and is heard through speakers or headphones.
In Other Words, Any digital information with speech or music stored on and played through a computer is known as an audio file or sound file. One of the most common types of audio file formats used today is the MP3. Clicking the triangular button on the following embedded player plays a short MP3 file in your browser.
